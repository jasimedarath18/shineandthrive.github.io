About Us

Shine & Thrive is a personal and professional development company that provides and facilitates legendry Bob Proctor’s time-tested, highly effective programs for individuals, couples, groups and corporates.

Bob Proctor and his business partner Sandy Gallagher aim to help people get in touch with their true self, understand their wants and capabilities; to bring their genius to the surface; to enable them live life to the fullest, effortlessly.

Bob Proctor

1934-2022
Tell me what you want, and I will show you how to get it.

Bob was born during the period of great depression and grew up during the WW2 era. Surrounded largely by lack and limitations, he grew up with a low self-image. But he went from down and out to changing the world! In 1961, Bob was guided by Ray Stanford, whom he considered as one of his earliest mentors, to study and follow the teachings of Napoleon Hill in the book Think and Grow Rich. This, Bob did religiously and went on to study the mind, human potential and himself for the rest of his life. He was inspired by another close mentor Earl Nightingale and was excited at the remarkable transformation of his own life. Thus, Bob dedicated his life to waking people up through his best- selling books, successful programs and events.

Thinking Into Results (TIR) is a wholesome program that has universal applicability. It is a one-of-a- kind system based on over 75 years of intensive research into the science and mechanics of personal achievement – what really makes successful people successful.

TIR is a highly-structured, step-by-step process that will empower you to design your future, fulfil your dreams and transform your life by going deeper into your mind and igniting change at that level because that is where your results are created. All other procedures would provide at the best temporary results. TIR addresses the root cause and works with the whole of you at Spiritual, Intellectual and Physical levels.

By living by the teachings of this program, you can –

  • Be more of the person you would really want to be.
  • Do more of the things you would really like to do.
  • Have more of the things you would really like to have.
  • Clarify your goal; Install it at the deepest levels of your mind; Harmonize it with the natural laws of the universe.
  • Enjoy a life of your dreams, no matter how far-fetched it might seem right now.

What makes TIR so effective –

  • Immediate Impact; it engages your mind at both conscious and sub-conscious levels.
  • Strategic Repetition; first and most important law of learning.
  • Lifelong Application and Access; we are designed to aspire for more, the desires would never diminish. This program can be applied time and again, in any area of your life, for the rest of your life.
